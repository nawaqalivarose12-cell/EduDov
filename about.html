🌐 What is EduDov?

EduDov is an online educational resource platform designed to help students learn in a simple, organized, and interactive way. It brings together quizzes, flashcards, study tools, and a community space where students can learn and support each other.

🎯 Why I Created EduDov

I created EduDov to help students access learning materials in one place. Many students struggle to find organized and reliable resources, so this platform was designed to make studying easier and more effective.

💡 Mission

The mission of EduDov is to support students by providing accessible, interactive, and engaging learning tools that improve understanding and academic performance.
